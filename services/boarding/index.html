Boarding Services

Murphy Veterinary has a simple Boarding model: With such a small number of spacious suites, each guest at Murphy Vet is assured a comfortable, personal stay. Murphy Vet's dogs leave their suites early in the morning and go out three to four time daily for extended periods. Cat litter boxes are changed daily.

Boarding Rates

  • $18 per night for dogs
  • $15 per night for cats

Boarding Benefits

  • Board for a single night or for extended periods
  • Day boarding $12: 8am to 6pm
  • Each pet receives hands-on attention and affection
  • Exercise, socialization, and interaction all day
  • Guests are encouraged to bring their favourite bedding, blankets, toys, and food
  • Sunday and Wednesday pick-up and drop-off available
  • We administer medications per your instructions for no cost
  • Pets are bathed for no cost

Boarding Requirements

All pets must have current vaccines; DOGs - Rabies, DHPP, and Bordatella (kennel cough). CATs - Rabies, FVRCP

Bath Rates

One dog bath without boarding: $29

One cat bath without boarding: $32
